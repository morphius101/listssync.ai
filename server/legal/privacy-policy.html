Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026  ·  Effective: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ListsSync.ai ("we," "us," or "our"), a product of Impact Development Consulting, collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our platform at https://listssync.ai (the "Service").

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Phone number (used solely for SMS verification and account security)
  • Billing information (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card numbers)

b. Marketplace & Business Information

  • Marketplace credentials and API keys you connect (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho)
  • Product data, listings, descriptions, images, and inventory information you upload or sync
  • Business name, GST/business registration info (if provided)

c. Usage Data

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
  • Device information: operating system, browser version
  • Feature usage patterns to improve the Service

d. Communications

  • Support tickets and messages you send to us
  • Feedback and survey responses

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account management: Creating and maintaining your account, verifying your identity
  • SMS verification: Sending one-time passwords (OTPs) and security alerts to your phone number via Twilio
  • Service delivery: Automating product listings across connected marketplaces on your behalf
  • Payments: Processing subscription and transaction payments via Stripe
  • Communications: Sending transactional emails (account alerts, invoice receipts) via SendGrid
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage to improve features, performance, and reliability
  • Legal compliance: Meeting obligations under applicable laws and regulations

3. SMS Communications

Message types: We send SMS messages only for account verification (OTPs) and critical account security notifications. We do not send marketing SMS messages.

Opt-in: You consent to SMS messages by entering your phone number during account registration and agreeing to our Terms of Service.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any SMS to unsubscribe. You can also disable SMS notifications in your account settings. Note: disabling SMS verification may limit account security features.

Help: Reply HELP to any SMS for assistance, or contact us at support@listssync.ai.

Frequency: Message frequency varies based on account activity. Message and data rates may apply.

SMS messages are delivered via Twilio, Inc. Twilio's privacy policy is available at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Marketplace platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho) as required to perform listing automation on your behalf — only data you explicitly provide or authorize
  • Service providers: Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (email), Firebase (authentication), Neon (database hosting) — under data processing agreements
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)

5. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Marketplace API keys are encrypted at rest
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel
  • We regularly review security practices and apply patches promptly

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Upon account deletion, we delete personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is consent-based)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@listssync.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and protect against CSRF attacks. We may use analytics cookies (e.g., page views) to understand how the Service is used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect Service functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us their data, please contact us immediately at privacy@listssync.ai.

10. International Data Transfers
